About Me

Born in Michigan in 1992.

After graduating from Western Michigan University in 2016, I was hired to do art direction for an advertising agency called Aisle Rocket Studies. I was there for just under two years, working with nearly a dozen clients—Whirlpool, Maytag, and KitchenAid to name a few. I learned web development on the job, quickly transitioning to becoming a junior web developer.

In 2018, after moving to a new agency (ddm marketing + communications), I've been responsible for working with clients in the healthcare, financial, industrial, and educational fields. Hired on as a frontend web developer, I've helped clients get their websites and designs from concept to launch for over two years.

Experience

Western Michigan University

Graphic Designer

Worked with the school to develop posters and designs advertising events around campus and elsewhere in Kalamazoo. This included working with the Gilmore Theatre, Frostic School of Art, the College of Engineering and Applied Sciences, the Design Center at Frostic, and the Kalamazoo Book Arts Center to create design solutions for our varied clients.

Aisle Rocket Studios

Art Director

Responsible for creating design solutions for Whirlpool, Kenmore, KitchenAid, and Maytag. Met with production and pre-production teams to ensure printed products included particular bells and whistles, like metallic inks, plastic packaging, and others. Required regular trips to retailers for point-of-purchase research.
