About Me

Born in Michigan in 1992.

After graduating from Western Michigan University in 2016, I was hired to do art direction for an advertising agency called Aisle Rocket Studies. I was there for just under two years, working with nearly a dozen clients—Whirlpool, Maytag, and KitchenAid to name a few. I learned web development on the job, quickly transitioning to becoming a junior web developer.

In 2018, after moving to a new agency (ddm marketing + communications), I've been responsible for working with clients in the healthcare, financial, industrial, and educational fields. Hired on as a frontend web developer, I've helped clients get their websites and designs from concept to launch for over two years.
